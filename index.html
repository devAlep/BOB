ABOUT US

Our storm prediction web app provides users with real-time information about severe weather events, including tornadoes, hurricanes, and thunderstorms. By combining weather data, computer models, and expert analysis, we are able to provide accurate and reliable predictions of where and when severe weather is likely to occur. Our goal is to help keep people safe and informed during severe weather events, so they can make better decisions about how to prepare for and respond to these potentially dangerous situations.

Our team is comprised of experienced meteorologists, data analysts, and web developers who are passionate about providing accurate and timely information about severe weather. We understand the importance of staying informed during severe weather events, and we believe that our storm prediction web app can help keep people safe and prepared. We are dedicated to using the latest weather data, computer models, and expert analysis to provide the most accurate storm predictions possible. Our team is committed to delivering the highest level of service to our users, and we welcome your feedback and suggestions as we continue to improve and expand our web app.
